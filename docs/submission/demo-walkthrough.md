# Demo Walkthrough

Follow these steps to evaluate the deployed CarbonCoach application.
Use an Incognito or clean browser profile for screenshots so browser extensions do not appear.

## 1. Open Deployed URL

Navigate to: https://carboncoach-192872123770.asia-south1.run.app/

## 2. Set up Profile

Complete the onboarding flow by answering lifestyle questions. Note that the profile persists locally in your browser and does not require an account.

## 3. View Footprint Summary

Review your estimated footprint breakdown. Notice the scientific assumptions and the deterministic nature of the calculation.

## 4. Review Recommendations & Action Plan

Check the recommended lower-impact choices based on your profile.

## 5. Trigger Footprint Coach

Click "Explain my footprint". Observe that the Gemini coach response appears only after you click, as it is user-triggered.

## 6. Open Daily Choice Lab

Navigate to the Daily Choice Lab to compare everyday alternatives (e.g., cab vs. metro).

## 7. Trigger Choice Coach

Ask for an explanation of a choice. The AI acts as a bounded coach to simplify the data.

## 8. Start Weekly Tracker

Select actions to track for the week.

## 9. Complete Weekly Actions

Check off completed actions and watch your local progress update.

## 10. View Carbon World Progression

Observe how completing actions visibly changes the Carbon World environment state.

## 11. Open Privacy & Local Data

Review the privacy page to confirm that no backend user database is used.

## 12. Open Estimates & Assumptions

Review the assumptions page to understand the deterministic logic behind the carbon estimates.

## 13. Clear Local Data

Click the option to clear your local data, which instantly resets the browser state.

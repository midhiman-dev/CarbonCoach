I'm incredibly proud to share **CarbonCoach**, my submission for the Prompt Wars Virtual Challenge! 🌱🚀

Most carbon footprint tools are just static calculators—they give you a number and leave you feeling overwhelmed. I built CarbonCoach to change that. It’s an AI-assisted awareness platform that helps you truly _understand_ your footprint and coaches you through everyday, actionable choices without the guilt trips.

Building this taught me some incredible lessons about AI safety and software engineering:
1️⃣ **Deterministic Calculation + AI Coaching:** The math is purely rule-based. We use Google Gemini exclusively for empathetic coaching and simplifying dense data.
2️⃣ **Numeric Invention Guard:** I built a custom validation layer to stop the LLM from fabricating savings or emission factors, ensuring the platform remains factual and trustworthy.
3️⃣ **Carbon World:** To make progress tangible, the app features an accessible visual progression of your environment—making sustainability engaging, not just a spreadsheet.
4️⃣ **Production Ready:** Built as a strict TypeScript monorepo, tested with 286 automated tests (~95% coverage), and deployed as a hardened, scalable Docker container on Google Cloud Run.
